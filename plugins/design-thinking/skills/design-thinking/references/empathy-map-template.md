# Empathy Map Template

## Structure

```markdown
### EM-{SEQ}: Empathy Map for PERSONA-{SEQ}

#### Says
- "{Direct quotes from the user about their experience}"
- "{What they tell colleagues/stakeholders}"

#### Thinks
- "{Internal thoughts they may not say aloud}"
- "{Concerns, doubts, hopes}"

#### Does
- {Observable actions and behaviors}
- {Workarounds they employ}
- {How they interact with current tools}

#### Feels
- {Emotional responses to current situation}
- {Frustrations, satisfactions, anxieties}

**Key Insight**: {One-sentence synthesis connecting all quadrants}
**Linked Pain Points**: PP-{SEQ}, PP-{SEQ}
```

## Quality Criteria

1. **All 4 quadrants must be filled** — Says, Thinks, Does, Feels
2. **Says vs Thinks gap** — Look for contradictions (says "it's fine" but thinks "this is broken")
3. **Does vs Feels gap** — Actions driven by negative emotions reveal design opportunities
4. **Minimum 2 entries per quadrant** for primary personas
5. Key Insight must synthesize across quadrants, not restate a single one
6. Every empathy map must link to at least one pain point
