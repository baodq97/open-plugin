# BA Competency Assessment Criteria

## Assessment Dimensions

### 1. Completeness
Does the work cover all necessary aspects?

| Level | Criteria |
|-------|---------|
| **L3** | Covers basic elements with some gaps. Follows templates without adaptation. |
| **L4** | Covers all required elements for the context. Adapts templates to fit the situation. Includes stakeholder input. |
| **L5** | Comprehensive coverage with proactive identification of gaps. Anticipates future needs. Integrates multiple perspectives. |
| **L6** | Sets the standard for completeness. Creates frameworks others can follow. Addresses organizational-level concerns. |

### 2. Depth
How deep is the analysis and thinking?

| Level | Criteria |
|-------|---------|
| **L3** | Surface-level analysis. Identifies obvious issues. Documents what is provided. |
| **L4** | Meaningful analysis with root cause thinking. Identifies non-obvious issues. Considers multiple options. |
| **L5** | Deep analysis with strategic implications. Evaluates systemic impacts. Provides authoritative guidance. |
| **L6** | Strategic depth across multiple domains. Identifies emerging patterns. Shapes organizational direction. |

### 3. Communication
How effectively is the work communicated?

| Level | Criteria |
|-------|---------|
| **L3** | Clear documentation for peers. Uses standard formats. Presents information logically. |
| **L4** | Adapts communication for technical and business audiences. Explains complex concepts clearly. Uses visuals effectively. |
| **L5** | Communicates with impact across stakeholder groups. Facilitates decision-making. Builds consensus. |
| **L6** | Communicates with authority at all organizational levels. Influences strategy. Promotes knowledge sharing. |

### 4. Decision Quality
How well are decisions made and justified?

| Level | Criteria |
|-------|---------|
| **L3** | Makes decisions within established guidelines. Documents decisions made. |
| **L4** | Makes evidence-based recommendations with clear trade-offs. Considers alternatives. Justifies choices with rationale. |
| **L5** | Makes strategic recommendations balancing competing stakeholder priorities. Manages uncertainty. Drives prioritization actively. |
| **L6** | Makes decisions that shape organizational strategy. Establishes decision-making frameworks. |

## Skill-Specific Assessment Indicators

### Business Situation Analysis (BUSA)

| Indicator | L3 | L4 | L5 |
|-----------|-----|-----|-----|
| Problem identification | Identifies obvious problems | Conducts root cause analysis, holistic view | Manages complex/ambiguous investigations |
| Stakeholder engagement | Engages under direction | Collaborates with operational stakeholders | Engages management-level stakeholders |
| Recommendations | Contributes to recommendations | Identifies and recommends improvements | Advises on approach and techniques |

### Requirements Definition (REQM)

| Indicator | L3 | L4 | L5 |
|-----------|-----|-----|-----|
| Elicitation | Follows standard techniques | Facilitates input, challenges constructively | Negotiates competing priorities |
| Scope management | Documents scope boundaries | Establishes baselines, manages changes | Drives prioritization for complex initiatives |
| Quality | Reviews for errors and omissions | Ensures traceability and agreement | Selects and adapts methods for context |

### Business Process Improvement (BPRE)

| Indicator | L3 | L4 | L5 |
|-----------|-----|-----|-----|
| Analysis | Applies standard techniques | Designs processes, identifies alternatives | Assesses feasibility of changes |
| Modelling | Documents processes | Develops graphical models | Selects and tailors methods |
| Improvement | Proposes improvements | Recommends implementation approaches | Manages execution of improvements |

### Feasibility Assessment (FEAS)

| Indicator | L3 | L4 | L5 |
|-----------|-----|-----|-----|
| Options analysis | Supports option identification | Identifies and short-lists options | Manages investigative work |
| Business cases | Supports cost/benefit identification | Prepares cost/benefit/risk analysis | Prepares full business cases |
| Stakeholder work | Gathers information | Engages internal/external stakeholders | Collaborates with specialists |

## Quick Assessment Checklist

For rapid assessment during a BA session:

### Analytical Thinking
- [ ] Identifies root causes, not just symptoms
- [ ] Considers multiple perspectives and options
- [ ] Supports recommendations with evidence
- [ ] Challenges assumptions constructively

### Requirements Quality
- [ ] Requirements are clear, measurable, and testable
- [ ] Acceptance criteria use Given/When/Then or equivalent
- [ ] Traceability to business objectives exists
- [ ] Scope boundaries are explicit

### Stakeholder Management
- [ ] Identifies all affected stakeholders
- [ ] Adapts communication for different audiences
- [ ] Facilitates decision-making (not just reporting)
- [ ] Manages conflicting stakeholder interests

### Process Analysis
- [ ] Documents current state (as-is) before proposing changes
- [ ] Identifies pain points with evidence
- [ ] Proposes improvements with clear rationale
- [ ] Considers impact on people, process, and technology

### Solution Design
- [ ] Evaluates multiple options before recommending
- [ ] Documents trade-offs clearly
- [ ] Defines measurable acceptance criteria
- [ ] Considers feasibility (technical, business, operational)
