# PO Competency Assessment Criteria

## Assessment Dimensions

### 1. Completeness
Does the work cover all necessary aspects?

| Level | Criteria |
|-------|---------|
| **L3** | Covers basic elements with some gaps. Follows templates without adaptation. |
| **L4** | Covers all required elements for the context. Adapts templates to fit the situation. Includes stakeholder input. |
| **L5** | Comprehensive coverage with proactive identification of gaps. Anticipates future needs. Integrates multiple perspectives. |
| **L6** | Sets the standard for completeness. Creates frameworks others can follow. Addresses organizational-level concerns. |

### 2. Depth
How deep is the analysis and thinking?

| Level | Criteria |
|-------|---------|
| **L3** | Surface-level analysis. Identifies obvious issues. Documents what is provided. |
| **L4** | Meaningful analysis with root cause thinking. Identifies non-obvious trade-offs. Considers multiple options. |
| **L5** | Deep analysis with strategic implications. Evaluates systemic impacts. Provides authoritative guidance. |
| **L6** | Strategic depth across multiple domains. Identifies emerging trends. Shapes organizational direction. |

### 3. Communication
How effectively is the work communicated?

| Level | Criteria |
|-------|---------|
| **L3** | Clear documentation for peers. Uses standard formats. Presents information logically. |
| **L4** | Adapts communication for technical and non-technical audiences. Explains complex concepts clearly. Uses visuals effectively. |
| **L5** | Communicates with impact across stakeholder groups. Facilitates decision-making. Builds consensus. |
| **L6** | Communicates with authority at all organizational levels. Influences strategy. Promotes knowledge sharing. |

### 4. Decision Quality
How well are decisions made and justified?

| Level | Criteria |
|-------|---------|
| **L3** | Makes decisions within established guidelines. Documents decisions made. |
| **L4** | Makes data-informed decisions with clear trade-offs. Considers alternatives. Justifies choices with rationale. |
| **L5** | Makes strategic decisions balancing competing stakeholder priorities. Manages uncertainty. Drives prioritization actively. |
| **L6** | Makes decisions that shape organizational strategy. Balances portfolio-level trade-offs. Establishes decision-making frameworks. |

## Skill-Specific Assessment Indicators

### Product Management (PROD)

| Indicator | L3 | L4 | L5 |
|-----------|-----|-----|-----|
| Backlog management | Assists with backlog tasks | Owns the backlog, prioritizes effectively | Adapts prioritization methods to context |
| Roadmap | Contributes to roadmap discussions | Creates and maintains roadmaps | Develops multi-segment strategies and positioning |
| Lifecycle management | Supports product activities | Manages elements of lifecycle | Manages full lifecycle including retirement |
| User insight | Collects and reports feedback | Uses insights to understand needs | Synthesizes multiple data sources for strategy |

### Requirements Definition (REQM)

| Indicator | L3 | L4 | L5 |
|-----------|-----|-----|-----|
| Elicitation | Follows standard techniques | Facilitates stakeholder input, challenges constructively | Negotiates competing priorities |
| Scope management | Documents scope boundaries | Establishes baselines, manages changes | Drives prioritization for complex initiatives |
| Traceability | Documents basic traceability | Ensures traceability to source | Manages scope across large initiatives |

### Stakeholder Management (RLMT)

| Indicator | L3 | L4 | L5 |
|-----------|-----|-----|-----|
| Engagement | Follows engagement plans | Implements engagement plans, collects feedback | Develops engagement strategies, acts as single POC |
| Communication | Reports to stakeholders | Communicates effectively to diverse audiences | Facilitates decision-making, builds consensus |
| Relationship building | Maintains assigned relationships | Develops and enhances relationships | Builds strategic long-term relationships |

### Delivery Management (DEMG)

| Indicator | L3 | L4 | L5 |
|-----------|-----|-----|-----|
| Methodology | Follows established processes | Applies appropriate methodologies | Adapts approaches based on context |
| Team leadership | Supports team coordination | Establishes and leads delivery teams | Provides leadership to multiple teams |
| Progress management | Tracks and reports progress | Communicates progress, risks, issues | Proactively manages cross-team dependencies |

## Quick Assessment Checklist

For rapid assessment during a PO session:

### Product Thinking
- [ ] Articulates clear user value for each feature
- [ ] Distinguishes between user needs and stakeholder wants
- [ ] Connects features to measurable outcomes
- [ ] Challenges scope actively (says "not yet" when appropriate)

### Prioritization
- [ ] Uses a consistent prioritization framework
- [ ] Justifies priority decisions with data or rationale
- [ ] Considers both user value and business impact
- [ ] Manages stakeholder expectations around priorities

### Communication
- [ ] Adapts message for different audiences
- [ ] Presents trade-offs clearly
- [ ] Facilitates decision-making (not just reporting)
- [ ] Listens to and incorporates feedback

### Strategic Thinking
- [ ] Connects tactical decisions to product vision
- [ ] Anticipates future needs and risks
- [ ] Considers competitive landscape
- [ ] Balances short-term delivery with long-term strategy

### Delivery
- [ ] Defines clear acceptance criteria
- [ ] Coordinates effectively with engineering
- [ ] Manages risks proactively
- [ ] Measures and iterates based on results
